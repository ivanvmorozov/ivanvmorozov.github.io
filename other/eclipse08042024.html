The Solar Eclipse of April 8th, 2024

Home

The sky darkens as it does at the edge of dusk and night, the horizon glows a deep red, Venus emerges as a bright dot, the silence of nature grows ever louder, and a sudden cold permeates the air. Everything comes to a stop for several minutes. A total solar eclipse is a majestic sight and experience to live through. It is very understandable why in the ancient times, our predecessors, who were not aware of orbital mechanics or the coincidental placement of the Sun and Moon that render them almost identical in observed size, created sinister explanations for this phenomenon.
